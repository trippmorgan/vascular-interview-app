# UNIVERSAL VASCULAR SURGERY TEMPLATE

**Use this as the base for all patient encounters.**

---

## PATIENT DEMOGRAPHICS
- Full name
- Date of birth / Age
- Gender
- Contact information (phone, address if mentioned)

---

## CHIEF COMPLAINT
**In patient's own words:**
- What brought the patient in today?
- How long has this been going on?
- What symptoms are most bothersome?

---

## CARDIOVASCULAR HISTORY

### Cardiac
- [ ] Heart attack (when, treatment received)
- [ ] Heart stents or bypass (when, how many vessels)
- [ ] Heart failure or other cardiac conditions
- [ ] Pacemaker or defibrillator

### Cerebrovascular
- [ ] Stroke (when, which side affected, residual deficits)
- [ ] TIA / mini-stroke (when, symptoms)
- [ ] Vision changes or episodes

### Vascular
- [ ] DVT / blood clots in legs (when, provoked or unprovoked)
- [ ] Pulmonary embolism (when)
- [ ] Peripheral arterial disease / claudication
- [ ] Previous vascular surgeries or procedures

### Risk Factors
- [ ] Hypertension (current BP control)
- [ ] High cholesterol (on statin?)
- [ ] Family history of vascular disease

## METABOLIC & SYSTEMIC CONDITIONS

### Diabetes
- [ ] Type 1 or Type 2
- [ ] Duration of diabetes
- [ ] Hemoglobin A1C (most recent value)
- [ ] Management: Insulin injections vs oral pills
- [ ] Complications: Retinopathy, nephropathy, neuropathy

### Kidney Disease
- [ ] CKD stage (if known)
- [ ] On dialysis? (type, how long, access location)
- [ ] Kidney function (creatinine if mentioned)

### Smoking History
- [ ] Current smoker, former smoker, or never smoked

## SURGICAL HISTORY
Document all prior surgeries:
- [ ] Knee or hip replacement/surgery
- [ ] Gallbladder removed (cholecystectomy)
- [ ] Appendix removed (appendectomy)
- [ ] Hysterectomy (if applicable)
- [ ] Obstetric history: Number of pregnancies, deliveries (C-section vs vaginal)
- [ ] Colonoscopy (results, polyps removed?)
- [ ] Any other abdominal, vascular, or major surgeries

---

## CURRENT MEDICATIONS

### Critical Vascular Medications
- [ ] **Antiplatelet**: Aspirin, Plavix (clopidogrel), Brilinta
- [ ] **Anticoagulation**: Warfarin (Coumadin), Eliquis, Xarelto, Lovenox
- [ ] **Statins**: Lipitor, Crestor, Zocor, Pravachol (cholesterol medication)

### Other Medications
- Blood pressure medications
- Diabetes medications (insulin, Metformin, etc.)
- Pain medications
- Any other relevant medications

### Medication Compliance
- Is patient taking medications as prescribed?
- Any side effects or tolerability issues?

---

## ALLERGIES
- Drug allergies (specific reaction: rash, anaphylaxis, GI upset, etc.)
- Contrast allergy (important for imaging)
- Latex allergy
- Other allergies

---

## SOCIAL HISTORY
- Living situation (alone, with family, assisted living)
- Occupation (if working, retired, disabled)
- Activity level and mobility
- Alcohol use
- Smoking status (if not already documented above)

---

## REVIEW OF SYSTEMS (Brief)
Document any positive findings mentioned:
- Constitutional: Fever, weight loss, fatigue
- Cardiovascular: Chest pain, palpitations, dyspnea
- Respiratory: Shortness of breath, cough
- Neurological: Weakness, numbness, dizziness
- Musculoskeletal: Joint pain, limited mobility
- Any other relevant symptoms
